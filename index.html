About The Event


“India's G20 Presidency will work to promote this universal sense of one-ness. Hence our theme - 'One Earth, One Family, One Future'” – Prime Minister , Narendra Modi


The G-20, is an international forum consisting of 19 countries and the European Union. The countries meet annually to discuss global economic issues and coordinate policy. The members account for around 80 percent of the world's economic output and two third of global population. The members aim to work together to promote economic growth. Every year, the leaders of G20 members meet to discuss mainly economic and financial matters and coordinate policy on some other issues of similar interest. The G20 is not a permanent institution with a headquarters, offices, or staff. Instead, its leadership rotates on an annual basis among its members, its decisions are made by a mutual compromise, and the implementation of its agenda depends on the political will of the individual states. The summit is fostering collaborative solutions that not only benefit its own population but contribute to the broader global well-being, reinforcing its spirit to a better world.

Bharat Mandapam serves as a “showcase and promoter of India as a top of the line global commercial enterprise vacation spot,” in line with a central authority assertion. F.A.Q

  • What is G20?

    The Group of Twenty (G20) is the premier forum for international economic cooperation. It plays an important role in shaping and strengthening global architecture and governance on all major international economic issues.

  • When and where did the first G20 session take place?

    G20 Session 1 was held in 2008 , Washington D.C.

  • Who is the chief coordinator of G20,India Presidency?

    Harsh Vardhan Shringla (born 1962) is Chief Coordinator for India's G20 Presidency in 2023.

  • How can today's youth contribute to G20?

    The youth can make a significant contribution by taking care of issues related to sustainable development, encouraging entrepreneurship, promoting creation and innovation, and giving this G20 a new perspective and noble ideas.
